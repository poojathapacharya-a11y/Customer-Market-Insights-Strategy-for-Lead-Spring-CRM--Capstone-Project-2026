Prototype Connection

ABS + WBA + pricing + recommendation logic

ABS shows the WA SME opportunity, WBA profiles supply real lead examples, ad-pricing benchmarks estimate campaign cost, and the scoring rules convert those inputs into package and channel recommendations.

Market growth evidence

WA reached 266,273 operating businesses in 2024-25, with 4.3% annual growth and more entries than exits.

Survival risk evidence

Only 64.1% of WA businesses operating in June 2021 survived to June 2025, supporting CRM and follow-up automation as a real need.

Segment priority evidence

Professional/IT, finance, health, property, and construction combine high small-business concentration with strong CRM relevance.
